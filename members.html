Meet the Members!

John Lennon

John Lennon was born in Liverpool on October 8th 1940. While he mostly played guitar, he did take-up piano in his later years. When Lennon divorced previous wife (Cynthia Powell), he married Yoko Ono who, along with George Harrison's wife Linda Eastman, encouraged Lennon not to depend on the other members of the band. When the Beatles broke up, Lennon continued writing music (a famous example would be the song Imagine) Unfortunatly on December 8th 1980, John Lennon was killed outside his home but his legacy and talent will never be forgotton.

George Harrison

Similar to Lennon, George Harrison was also born in Liverpool. Harrison was known as the "quiet Beatle", writing more "experimental" songs for the band including Here Comes The Sun, While My Guitar Gently Weeps and Taxman. While it was mostly band members Lennon and McCartney writing songs, each album from 1967 contained at least a couple of McCartneys work. Harrison was heavily inspired by Indian and Hindu traditions (as seen with the use of the sitar in many songs). Unfortunatly in 2001, Harrison passed away at the age of 58 from lung cancer.

Paul McCartney

Paul McCartney, born in Liverpool on June 18th 1940, was the main bass player of the Beatles. Along with John Lennon, he provided the main vocals however he was also self-taught in piano, guitar and songwriting. When he was 15, McCartney met John Lennon and the two, along with George Harrison and Lennons friend Stuart Sutcliffe, formed a band known as the Quarrymen. After Sutcliffe left the band, McCartney replaced him as the bass player.

Ringo Starr

Ringo Starr was born on July 7th, 1940 and went on to become a singer, songwriter and an actor. While he mostly served as the drummer for the band, he also provided lead vocals to songs such as With A Little Help From My Friends and Yellow Submarine. However, Starr originally found some success playing in a previous band called Rory Storm and the Hurricanes before leaving the band to join the Beatles. Ringo Starr has also gone on to pursue acting, in movies such as The Last Waltz, The Magic Christian, and Son of Dracula.
